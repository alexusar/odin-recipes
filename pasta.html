Bolognese Pasta

This is the amazing Bolognese Pasta, hand crafted by the master chef Gordon Ramsey.

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef
  • Spaghetti
  • Thyme
  • Sugar
  • Tomato
  • Bay Leaves
  • Onion
  • Garlic

Recipe:

Steps on How to Make the Bolognese Sauce:

  1. Saute garlic and onion (about 3 minutes)
  2. Brown the beef (about 2 minutes)
  3. Add the sugar, thyme, tomato, and bay leaves. Give it a good stir then simmer for 20 minutes minimum

Steps on How to Make the Spaghetti:

  1. Put a large pot with water to boil
  2. Add the Spaghetti and let it cook (about 10 minutes depending on the spaghtetti)
