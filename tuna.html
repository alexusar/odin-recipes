Arroz con Atun y Huevo

This is Arroz con Atun y Huevo, or in english, rice with tuna and eggs.

Ingredients:

  • 2 or 3 cans of tuna
  • 2 cups of rice
  • 4 eggs
  • salt
  • olive oil

Recipe:

  1. Cook the 2 cups of rice in a pot
  2. Hard boil 4 eggs and cut them when they are finished
  3. Once the the eggs and the rice are finished, put everything into a pot, inlcuding the tuna, salt, and olive oil
